## Personalización Guidelines

### Research requerido:
1. Recent news/updates de la empresa
2. Industry challenges específicos  
3. Competitor analysis y positioning
4. Decision maker background via LinkedIn
5. Company size/stage para adaptar approach

### Success Metrics:
- Open rate: >25%
- Response rate: >12%  
- Meeting booking rate: >20% of responses
- Conversion rate: >30% of meetings to opportunity
